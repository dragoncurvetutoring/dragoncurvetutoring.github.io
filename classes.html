MATHCOUNTS

MATHCOUNTS will cover intermediate concepts of olympiad level math, and dive deeper into high-level mathematic problems. This class will help prepare you for the Mathcounts competition series. We will be using materials provided direction from MATHCOUNTS. This class is suited for students in grades 6-8.

Details

Student to teacher ratio : 3:1
Classes : 6 classes, two week sessions (3 classes a week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.

Mathematical Exploration & Opportunities for Women

Mathematical Exploration & Opportunities for Women is an introductory research camp for girls. During the camp, students will learn about advanced math topics, like graph theory, cryptography, and fractals. The camp will culminate in a group final project and presentation. This camp is suited for students in grades 6-8.

Details

Student to teacher ratio : 6:1
Sessions : 10 sessions, two week camp (5 classes a week, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday)
Camp Day Length: 4 hours (1PM to 5pm)
