ELMO will cover basic olympiad level concepts, teach students the fundamentals of Algebra, Geometry, Number Theory, and Combinatorics, and prepare students for elementary school competitions such as Math Kangaroo, MOEMS, and inteGIRLS. This class is suited for students in grades 3-6.

Student to teacher ratio : 3:1
Classes : 6 classes, two week sessions (3 classes a week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.

MATHCOUNTS will cover intermediate concepts of olympiad level math, and dive deeper into high-level mathematic problems. This class will help prepare you for the Mathcounts competition series. We will be using materials provided direction from MATHCOUNTS. This class is suited for students in grades 6-8.

Student to teacher ratio : 3:1
Classes : 6 classes, two week sessions (3 classes a week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.

AMCS will cover intermediate and advanced topics in olympiad level math, and help teach strategies specifically for math competitions. This class is used to help prepare students for the AMC8/10 competition. Furthermore, we will be providing free copies of our own textbooks to be used. This class is suited for students in grades 6-8.

Student to teacher ratio : 3:1
Classes : 6 classes, two week sessions (3 classes a week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.

Our Science Bowl program helps prepare middle schoolers for the National Science Bowl competition. This course will cover basic physics, chemistry, and biology topics. This class is suitable for students in grades 6-8.

Student to teacher ratio : 6:1
Classes : 9 classes, three week sessions (3 classes a week, Tuesday, Wednesday, and Friday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.

Introduction to CAD is a one week course that will teach students how to use Onshape to model in 3d. At the end of this class, each student will design their own product and get it 3d printed. This class is suited for students in grades 5+. You will need a computer for this class.

Student to teacher ratio : 6:1
Classes : 4 classes, one week intensive (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
Class Length: 1 hour

Classes meet at the Rockville Memorial Library - 21 Maryland Ave, Rockville, MD 20850.
