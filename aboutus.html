Who are we?

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing math and programming tutoring to underserved communities. Our goal is to bridge the educational gap by empowering individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in these fields. We believe that education is a transformative force and strive to make a lasting impact by offering personalized tutoring, workshops, and collaborative projects. Our inclusive community fosters peer learning and creativity, while aiming to create equal access to quality education for all.

Why Dragon Curve?

We chose the name "Dragon Curve" for its representation of the complexity and beauty found in mathematics.

The Dragon Curve, also known as the Heighway Dragon, is a fractal curve generated by a simple algorithm. Despite its simple generation, the curve has complex and intricate patterns that can be explored in depth. The curve is a fascinating representation of mathematical concepts and is often used as a visual aid in teaching math and programming. By choosing this name, we aim to inspire our students to see the beauty in math and programming and to approach these subjects with curiosity and wonder.

Our Mission

At Dragon Curve Tutoring, we strive to make high-quality math and programming education accessible to all, especially underserved communities. Our mission is to provide equal opportunities for everyone to learn and grow, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that education is a powerful tool that can transform lives and open doors to new opportunities.

To achieve this, we offer personalized tutoring to our students. Our experienced tutors work with each student to identify their strengths and weaknesses and create a learning plan that meets their individual needs. We understand that everyone learns differently, and that's why we take a personalized approach to teaching.
