Support Dragon Curve Tutoring

Your donation can make a profound difference in the lives of individuals from underserved communities. At Dragon Curve Tutoring, we provide high-quality math and programming education to those who need it most. By contributing, you help us bridge the educational gap and empower students with essential skills for success.

Donations help fund math competition teams, provide better class materials, create Dragon Curve merchandise, and improve our learning environment. Every dollar supports personalized tutoring, engaging workshops, and collaborative projects that foster creativity and peer learning.

You can support us through multiple donation methods:

  • PayPal: Use our PayPal link to make a secure online donation.
  • Zelle: Send your donation via Zelle using our email address info@dragoncurvetutoring.org
  • Employer Donation Programs: Search for our non-profit organization name "Dragon Curve Tutoring Inc" if you are donating through your company's matching or donation program.

As a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, every donation makes an impact, and we are so thankful for your support. No goods or services were exchanged for your contribution, and your gift is tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. Donation acknowledgment letters will be provided to each donor, which can be used for tax purposes. Please retain this letter for your tax records.

Join us in our mission to create equal access to education, and help us inspire the next generation to find beauty and wonder in mathematics and programming. Your generosity can transform lives and build a brighter future for all.
